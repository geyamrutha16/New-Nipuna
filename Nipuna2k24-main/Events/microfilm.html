Brain Teasers

About


Brain teasers are puzzles or riddles that are designed to challenge one's cognitive abilities, such as problem-solving and logical reasoning.


They come in various forms and difficulty levels and often require creative thinking to solve.


Brain teasers can be verbal, mathematical, visual, or even physical in nature.

Rules


1 . Silence During Questions: Stay quiet while quiz questions are being read aloud to allow all participants to hear and focus on the questions without distraction.


2 . No Answers or Hints: Refrain from blurting out answers or providing hints to participants while questions are being asked. Allow participants to think and respond on their own.
